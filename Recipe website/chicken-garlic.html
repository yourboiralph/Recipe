CHICKEN GARLIC

Ingredients
  • 1 ½ lbs. chicken thighs
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups cooking oil
  • 2 tablespoons Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup cornstarch
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup cornstarch
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • ½ cup Ladys Choice Mayonnaise
  • ½ teaspoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon Knorr Liquid Seasoning
Procedures
  • Combine chicken with the chicken seasoning ingredients. Mix well. Let it stay for 10 minutes.
  • Meanwhile combine flour mixture ingredients. Mix well and set aside.
  • Heat 2 cups of cooking oil in a deep cooking pot.
  • While the oil is heating-up, dredge the chicken in flour mixture. Dip in beaten egg, and then dredge once more with the flour mixture.
  • Deep-fry the chicken for 14 minutes or until golden brown. Remove chicken from the cooking pot. Set aside.
  • Make the Mayo Garlic glaze by melting butter on a clean pan. Add garlic. Cook until it starts to brown. Turn the heat off and add Lady’s Choice Mayonnaise, lemon juice, honey, and Knorr Liquid Seasoning. Mix well.
  • Add the crispy fried chicken and toss with the glaze until completely coated.
  • Transfer to a serving plate. Serve. Share and enjoy!
